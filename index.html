CSK Artificial Intelligence for In Silico Drug Discovery
Our platform provides necessary technologies from target identification to new drug design. The platform is freely accessible for accurate and fast drug discovery.

Free access

The platform is open to the public. Any scientists can use our platform for target identification, drug design, and ADME-Tox prediction.

All-in-one system

The platform provides various technologies: omics-based target identification, knowledge-based target identification, hepatotoxicity prediction, blood-brain-barrier permeability prediction, cardiotoxicity prediction, and drug design.

Neurodegenerative diseases

The platform is specialized for drug discovery of neurodegenerative diseases including as Alzheimer’s and Huntington’s diseases. However, the platform is also designed for conventional drug discovery.

Opening

We open our in silico AI-based drug discovery platform and aim at providing an easy-to-use all-in-one drug discovery platform, who feel uncomfortable with various parameters, options, etc.

Drug Discovery Platform

Our platform is able to identify and study prime target proteins for diseases, design potential drug candidates, and predict their potential toxicity in liver and heart. The in silico platform would facilitate the discovery of new drugs.

Collaboration

The platform was developed in collaboration with University - AI Company - Institute (Chung-Ang University, Standigm, and KRICT). Chung-Ang University developed a target identification module based omics data and ADME-Tox prediction modules, Standigm developed a drug design module and a knowledge-based target identification, and KRICT provided experimental data and evaluated the performances of our models.
